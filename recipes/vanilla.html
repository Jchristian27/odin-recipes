Vanilla Ice Cream

Vanilla ice cream is a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla that has been frozen into a soft, creamy delight using special techniques.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients, stirring to dissolve sugar completely.
  2. Fill cylinder of ice cream maker no more than two-thirds full; freeze according to manufacturer's directions. (Refrigerate any remaining mixture until ready to freeze.)
  3. Serve immediately or store in covered containers in freezer.
